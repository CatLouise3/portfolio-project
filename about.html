About Me

I'm Catherine McFee


I am currently working on my bachelors in Education, but I have always been interested in web design and the idea of coding. Due to 2020's circumstances, it allowed me to have the time to start expanding my horizons and trying something new. I started my coding journey in May 2021 and haven't stopped since.

I started with the basics and got even more interested as time went on. My studies included learning HTML, CSS, Javascript, and more! When I am not coding, I am working on my degree and working as an educator. My goal is to graduate and continue on this technology and coding journey as I continue to grow and learn!
